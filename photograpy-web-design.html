Architectural Photography Web Design

Design of a web page in Adobe xd for an architectural photography company, based on straight lines, polygons and triangles inspired by their photographs.
White, black and blue color range, with semi-transparent navigation bar, to denote neatness and professionalism.
Typography with straight edges and translucent fill representative of the architectural lines and delineated plans.
It consists of home, services, portfolio divided into exterior and interior photography and contact pages.
The portfolio gallery has cards and geometric designs according to each image, which are ordered by color range and objects, to provide a homogeneous and ordered image of the company's work.
Create a clean, modern brand identity committed to architecture, taking full advantage of the spaces they photograph.
